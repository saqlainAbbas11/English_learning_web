About Me

My Personal Journey

My love for the English language began when I was just a child. I was fascinated by how words could create entire worlds in literature. This passion led me to pursue a degree in English Literature and later to share this love through teaching.

Over the past 10 years, I've had the privilege of teaching students from diverse backgrounds and age groups, each with their unique learning needs and goals.

My Teaching Philosophy

Student-Centered Approach

I believe every student learns differently. My lessons are tailored to individual learning styles, ensuring maximum engagement and retention.

Real-World Application

Language learning shouldn't be confined to textbooks. I incorporate real-life scenarios, current events, and cultural elements into my lessons.

Hobbies & Interests

Reading

I'm an avid reader, particularly of classic literature and contemporary fiction. Some of my favorite authors include Jane Austen and Khaled Hosseini.

Travel

Exploring new cultures and meeting people from different backgrounds enriches my teaching and broadens my perspective.

Cooking

I love experimenting with recipes from around the world, especially traditional Azerbaijani dishes and international cuisine.
